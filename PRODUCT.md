# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Chinese learners (college students, young professionals) practicing English speaking on their own, typically on a desktop browser. Primary context: focused practice sessions of 5-15 minutes. They want immediate feedback without breaking conversation flow.

## Product Purpose

AnytimeSpeak is an AI English speaking coach. Users pick a real-life scenario (interview, restaurant, meeting), have a voice or text conversation with an AI partner, receive turn-by-turn grammar and expression feedback, and end with a scored session summary. Success means a user finishes a session feeling they improved and can see where.

## Brand Personality

Warm, encouraging, playful. "Your best friend who happens to be an English tutor." Inspired by Taylor Swift Lover-era aesthetics: soft pinks, lavender, dreamy gradients, never harsh or clinical. Chinese-first interface; English is the practice content, not the UI language.

## Anti-references

- Cold blue SaaS dashboards (Notion, Linear feel)
- Harsh neon "gamification" (Duolingo green-overload)
- Sterile white medical / enterprise forms
- Overly serious language-school aesthetics

## Design Principles

1. Practice flows uninterrupted — feedback lives beside the conversation, never in front of it.
2. Warm confidence — the UI should feel like encouragement, not evaluation.
3. Show the score last — do not flash numbers before the session ends.
4. One screen, one job — each view has a primary action; no competing CTAs.
5. Voice-first with text fallback — microphone is the default; typing is always available.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Support reduced-motion preferences. Ensure sufficient contrast on the pink/lavender palette (body text against tinted backgrounds). Chinese and English mixed text must render legibly at all sizes.
